TOODLES

About Toodles

Toodles joined our family about one year ago. We adopted him, and took him to get all of his shots. He is like our child. Our fur baby.


Fashion

He enjoys wearing fashionable outfits. He gets a few new outfits for every season.

Activities

Enjoys running and playing outside

Recent Events

Recently he was hit by a car while running in the street. He bounced off the bumper and recovered quickly.

Aftermath

He became angry at anything with wheels. He hated skateboards, wheelchairs, scooters, bicycles etc. He would chase them and bark.

Therapy

We took him to a veterinarian psychiatrist who recommended audio therapy. He is now calmer and does not chase after, growl bark or snap at random wheels.
